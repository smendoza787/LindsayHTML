Makeup Artist

I was born in New York and raised in Las Vegas.

I consider myself a diversely artistic person, with strong influences in both beauty makeup and makeup effects.

I believes that makeup isn't just a new face to put on, but an enhancement of ones features that they can feel confident wearing. I use only the best products and have extensive and up-to-date product knowledge, so I'll be sure you know what product is being applied and why.

So if you are searching for a passionate, creative, and understanding artist look no further!
